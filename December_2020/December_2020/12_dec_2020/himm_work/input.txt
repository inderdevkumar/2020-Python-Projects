Selenium is an umbrella project for a range of tools and libraries that enable and support the automation of web browsers. This project is made possible by volunteer contributors who have put in thousands of hours of their own time, and made the source code freely available for all users. Selenium brings together browser vendors, engineers, and enthusiasts to further an open discussion around automation of the web platform .
